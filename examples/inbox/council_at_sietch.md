# War council — Sietch Tabr

Present: Muad'Dib, Stilgar, the Fedaykin commanders, the Lady Jessica, Gurney Halleck

The question put to the council: the Fremen are ready to carry the holy war off-world. Do we loose it, and if not, what instead?

- Stilgar: the legions are past holding. Give the word and they take the Imperium for you.
- Gurney: a war that size depends on transport and spice we do not yet fully control; move too early and the Guild strands us.
- Jessica: the fervor was seeded by the sisterhood; it can be aimed, but it will not stay aimed.
- A younger naib: then refuse it — walk into the desert and let the prophecy starve.
- Another: or turn it. Spend the faith on Kynes' dream and green this planet instead of burning a thousand others.

Options on the table: unleash the jihad, restrain the legions, abdicate the throne, or channel the fervor into terraforming.

Action: Muad'Dib decides. The Fremen will not wait long.
