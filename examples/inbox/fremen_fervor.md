# Sietch report — the legions are ready

From: Stilgar, Naib of Sietch Tabr

Muad'Dib. The Fedaykin wait on one word from you. Not a season's wait — a day's.

Every sietch from the south now flies the green and black. They do not call you Duke. They call you Mahdi, the Lisan al-Gaib, the voice from the outer world. The young men sharpen crysknives and speak of carrying your name to the stars.

I have led raids my whole life. I have never seen fervor like this, and I have never seen anything stop it once it is loosed. They will march with you. If you fall, they will march for your memory. Understand what you are holding: not an army, a faith.

Give the word and Arrakis empties into the galaxy.
