# The vision — what prescience shows

Across every path the prescience opens, the same future waits: green-and-black banners on a
thousand worlds, the Fremen jihad burning outward from Arrakis, and at the end of the counting,
sixty-one billion dead.

The terrible thing is not that one path leads there. It is that they all do. Restrain the
legions and the war finds another shape. Walk away and they march for a martyr. The whole
Imperium's fate depends on a choice made here, and the risk cannot be unseen once it is seen.

The only variable the vision will not fix is *you* — whether you ride at the head of the storm,
or are crushed somewhere beneath it. That part is still a decision.
