# A warning, mother to son

From: the Lady Jessica

You should know whose hand is already on this.

The Missionaria Protectiva seeded these prophecies on Arrakis centuries ago — a safety net of faith for a stranded sister to use. The Fremen belief that you are the Mahdi is not entirely theirs. It was *built*, and you are standing inside the machine.

That cuts two ways. The faith is real enough now to move millions — and it is also a thing engineered to be steered. The danger is believing you are the one steering. A religion built as a tool does not stay a tool; it wakes up, and then it uses the one it was pointed at.

Whatever you choose, choose it knowing the fervor was loaded long before you arrived.
