# Threat assessment — what moves if Muad'Dib moves

From: the war-mentat's tally

Read the board cold before the council romanticizes it.

- **The Emperor** keeps the Sardaukar in reserve and will spend them the instant a Fremen army leaves Arrakis.
- **The Great Houses of the Landsraad** fear a messiah more than they fear each other; a holy war unites them against you.
- **The Spacing Guild** — the external power that moves every army between worlds — can strand a jihad in place by refusing transport, unless we hold the spice over them.
- **The Bene Gesserit** will disown the prophecy they seeded the moment it stops serving them.

Every one of these is an enemy the moment the legions march. The single lever that holds them all is the spice — and only as long as the threat to it is believed.
