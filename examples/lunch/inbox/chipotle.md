# Chipotle — the usual bowl

From: the group chat

The default pick. Chicken bowl, brown rice, fajita veggies, mild plus medium. Roughly $11
after tax, and the ingredients are fresh and grilled to order — far from the most processed
thing on this list.

Filling enough that nobody's starving by 3pm, and a decent portion for the price. The
catch: the line at noon is brutal, ten to fifteen minutes of it — and we'd be repeating it
again.
