# The taquería down the block

From: the group chat

The family taquería two blocks over — a five-minute walk, no line at lunch. The carne asada
burrito is about $9, made fresh to order, and the portion is genuinely huge. Real food,
basically nothing processed.

We almost never go, so it'd be something different. Only catch: cash is easier than card,
and the walk eats a few minutes the drive-thru wouldn't.
